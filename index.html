About Us

Amazing FBA, led by Michael Veazey & Nicolas Kalmus, is an E-Commerce Consultancy & Set of Communities.

The main focus is now on  6- and 7-figure UK  or European Amazon Private Label Sellers/Brand Owner-Operators.

 While focussed on UK and Europe, we've had clients from Melbourne, Australia, and Colorado, USA.  

We help eCommerce/Amazon private label sellers & Brand Owners:

  • Dominate their markets
  • Drive up their profits
  • Create a 7-figure sellable business

We deliver:

  • One-to-one tailored consultancy
  • Structured Training from World-Class Experts
  •  Masterminds of highly motivated Amazon entrepreneurs

We believe:

  •  That “Star” positioning is the bedrock of e-commerce success.
  • That profit (NOT revenue) is the real driver of business value
  • That close relationships with your true peers massively drive business growth and enjoyment.
