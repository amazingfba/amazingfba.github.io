What we do

Intimate groups, in Person

We meet in person in London multiple times a year. Group sizes are limited to 12 people max in the room. 

The main focus is the hot-seat, where you're guaranteed to have focus on you and your personal business each time.

We also meet on Zoom on in-between months. Again in really small groups. We include expert-led sessions, which have included: intellectual property law, selling your business and annual planning, among many others. 

In-person

Online is lonely. Online gets overwhelming. 
In person means connection at a human level. And finally making sense of the info you've got.

Small Groups

There is no shortage of cheer-leading big groups in person in the industry. Nor of mass webinars. We keep it tiny for trust, and getting to real know each other. 

Interactive for you personally

Who needs yet another webinar or Youtube video? What matters isn't info any more. It's how you decide to apply it yourself. In your business. That's where we shine.
